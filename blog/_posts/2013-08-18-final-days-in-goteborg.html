I am having a hard time telling where the last three weeks have gone. Needless too say, it has been a great time. I am a little sad to go on Tuesday, but excited for this new chapter and journey I am undertaking. I will miss my family, and it always feels heavy to leave. Especially this time since I don’t know when I will see everyone again.

The past few weeks I have done my Sweden Tour which has included Svartå, Storfors and Karlstad. It has been wonderful to see my family and spend some quality time. I have met Alfred Nobel, who is looking really good for his age. I can now tell you why he is really important for Sweden and his history. If it ever comes up on Trivia night, shoot me a text.

This weekend I got to spend some time with mom and Lars. I also got lucky to catch the tail end of the Göteborg Culture Fest, with Anna and Gustav. We caught the big concert, with lots of different Swedish artists. Picture is below. I got to see Loreen who won the Eurovision contest last year. So that was pretty sweet. Euuuuuuphoria!!!

Tomorrow I will be packing and trying to figure out how to get everything to the states. It will be interesting, and I am hoping Delta will be lenient. Ha! There is a lot of jam. And books. A few red bricks. Stones and other heavy things. The normal stuff.

I remember daydreaming all of April, may, and June waiting on this vacation. Now it is almost over. Time flies so enjoy every moment you are given. I know I have, and I still have much to look forward to on my road trip out west.

Lina out!!!
