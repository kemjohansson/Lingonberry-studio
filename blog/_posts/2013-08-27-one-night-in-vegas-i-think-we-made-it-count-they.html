One night in Vegas. I think we made it count! They had the biggest Chahuly sculpture in the Bellagio that I have ever seen! It was incredibly beautiful. Sadly I had a huge fall out with a pair of close friends.  They were just wearing me down, and made the whole night simply unbearable.  I thought I could make it through the evening without saying anything, but finally I had to stop and tell Kimberly I had enough.  My shoes were slowly tearing my feet right off.  We flagged down a guard in MGM Grand and got bandaids.  I was probably not the first woman who has broken up with her shoes in a casino, since he had some right in his pocket.  Noted for next time.
